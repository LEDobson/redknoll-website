Lungs

Lungs is an air purification system built around a Neptune 4 Pro laser cutter. It’s designed to filter out harmful particles and gases from the air, creating a cleaner and safer environment in the workshop. Lungs uses a combination of HEPA filters, activated carbon filters, and UV‑C light to capture and neutralize contaminants. The system is modular, allowing for easy maintenance and upgrades as needed. With Lungs in place, I can work on projects that generate dust or fumes without worrying about air quality.
